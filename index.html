ABOUT

Rosé

Rosé is a Korean-New Zealand singer and dancer. She was born on February 11, 1997 in Auckland, New Zealand and trained at YG Entertainment for four years before debuting with Blackpink in 2016.

In addition to working with Blackpink, Rosé also released her first solo album, "R" in 2021. The album received huge success, topping the Billboard 200 chart and becoming the solo album of a the first Korean artist to achieve this achievement.

Rosé is one of the most famous K-pop artists in the world. She has a large number of fans globally and is considered a fashion icon.

"Rosé is known for her powerful voice and unique timbre. She has been dubbed the "golden voice" of K-pop and is praised by music critics. complimented me on my ability to sing live."
